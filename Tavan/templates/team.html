Mr. Rajendra Lokwani (Managing Director)

He is Civil Engineering graduate of 1980 and has extensive Design and Execution experience in all facets of Civil Construction with particular focus on Irrigation Projects, Railway and Road -Bridges. After graduation he has served for about 33 years with Madhya Pradesh Water Resources Department. During his services he has designed many Major, Medium and Minor Irrigation projects. He has been posted in field and during filed services, he was in charge of construction of medium and Minor concrete and earthen dam. He was also in charge of Sip Kolar link project, where he has executed 3800 m tunnel in 17 months. He has having a reach experience in designing of Concrete dam, earthen dam , Open irrigation system ,Pressurized irrigation system , Lift Irrigation projects , Tunnel designing , Railway structures like major and minor bridges ,Road works and Road bridges.
