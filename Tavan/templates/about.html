About Us

Tavan Infratech

Tavan Infratech is promoted by experienced entrepreneur and is headed by Mr. Rajendra Lokwani. He is Civil Engineering graduate of 1980 and has extensive Design and Execution experience in all facets of Civil Construction with particular focus on Irrigation Projects.

After graduation he has served for about 33 years with Madhya Pradesh Water Resources Department and took VRS in September 2014 to join Tavan Infratech, promoted by Mrs. Chandna Lokwani in year 2010. She is Post graduate in Math's discipline and is responsible for overall management of the company.

She is also well versed with CADD and other operating systems and has been working on various projects in individual capacity for more than ten years for Survey and Design activities for Irrigation, Roads and Railway projects.

Tavan Infratech was reconstituted as Limited Liability partnership in January 2017, Directors being Mr. Rajendra Lokwani, Mrs. Chandna Lokwani and Ms. Tanya Lokwani.
